Services

PROGRAMS, EVENTS, CAMPAIGNS & PROJECTS

We learn about food and agricultural sector updates, focus on challenges and Opportunities. We plan and manage programs, events, campaigns and projects to support national goals and policies

RESEARCH & DATA COLLECTION

We provide independent data collection, food and agricultural intelligence reporting services that can underpin decision-making on food and agricultural investments, new products, services and much more.

CONTENT & DOCUMENTATION

We reproduce food and agricultural materials and contents. We make it available to readers, Organizations, Storage, Retrieval, and Dissemination

MARKETING & SALES

We are specialist in food and agricultural marketing and sales. We help farmers and livestock keepers to access market at both national & international levels.

FOOD & AGRICULTURE VOLUNTEERS

We help local farming communities to achieve goals, people from abroad can join our volunteer programs that aim at impacting food and agriculture sector.
